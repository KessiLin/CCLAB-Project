About Christmas Eve:
"Enjoy your lonely Christmas"

This is a song of loneliness.
The narrator tells his story of a failed relationship.
The vibe is shadow especially it is a song called Christmas Eve.
The lyrics are straightforward but very descriptive.

I inserted some relative images,
modified texts' colors and spacing,
and centered the whole lyrics,
so that the audience could feel the feelings of loneliness and vanishing relationships that the lyrics describe.
